1. Install the MCP Inspector from the [official docs](https://modelcontextprotocol.io/docs/tools/inspector)
2. Now just run this mcp-inspector & get the local url. Simple as this: <img width="651" height="120" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c7a75cc0-f1a8-4252-afd8-91bb651383e9" />
3. Now just go to this url, make sure to select `Streamable HTTP` for `Transport Type` from the top left <img width="1330" height="651" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/10b505e9-c200-4819-8ed1-faf7237a2ff3" />
4. Provide `https://postcrawl.com/mcp` in the `URL` field: <img width="1366" height="623" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0f934c3a-ac3b-4493-8187-2a9140ade98a" />. **REMEMBER IT SHOULD BE https://postcrawl.com/mcp and not https://edge.postcrawl.com/mcp as shown in the images here**
5. Now expand the `Authentication`, just click here: <img width="1346" height="643" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a9aad994-4bcc-4af9-a9bf-cc1999f4cb84" />
6. Now go to [PostCrawl platform](https://platform.postcrawl.com/ideaape-pll49AK/settings/api-keys) and create an API key like this : <img width="1316" height="698" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/daa03164-fa5c-42ec-aabf-42087b7921b4" />
7. Copy that API Key and paste that verbatim in Bearer Token, also add `Authorization` as it is in the `Header Name` : <img width="1320" height="626" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5b8c7aab-94b2-412a-b62a-eae0c45c7d09" />
8. Click connect and it's good to go: <img width="1322" height="648" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a7e9c40c-e560-4b91-91a3-d61776c82269" />
9. Once connected, you can see the tools by clicking here: <img width="1169" height="549" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2a35cc02-a2c3-4a41-9217-bbcce017d82d" />
10. Click on any of the tools from here: <img width="1005" height="609" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a14f5727-8011-4cab-baf0-30ef5f591649" />
11. Just provide your query, and make sure to provide the social_platforms: <img width="966" height="532" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/264a9b61-139d-4a73-8b93-3d7e53eda503" />
12. Click `run_tool` and bang! :rocket:

**AGAIN REMEMBER TO KEEP https://postcrawl.com/mcp AND NOT https://edge.postcrawl.com/mcp IN THE URL FIELD**
